# Iran war: Trump says US will 'watch over' Strait of Hormuz

**Priority:** HIGH

US President Donald Trump said the vital Strait of Hormuz "must be open to everyone" and "no one can control it." He made a threat to Oman, which is negotiating with Iran to control the vital waterway.
