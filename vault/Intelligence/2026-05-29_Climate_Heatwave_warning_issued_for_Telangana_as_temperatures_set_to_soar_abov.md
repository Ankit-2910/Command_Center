# Heatwave warning issued for Telangana as temperatures set to soar above 44°C

**Priority:** MEDIUM

The forecast stated that Adilabad, Bhadradri Kothagudem and Khammam are expected to record temperatures above 44°C on May 29, while Kumram Bheem Asifabad and Mancherial are also likely to experience similar conditions on both May 29 and May 30
