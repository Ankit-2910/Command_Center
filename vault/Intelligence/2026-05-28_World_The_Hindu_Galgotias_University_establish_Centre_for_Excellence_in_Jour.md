# The Hindu, Galgotias University establish Centre for Excellence in Journalism

**Priority:** LOW

Select students of Galgotias University will gain access to internship opportunities with The Hindu’s editorial teams, collaborative publishing opportunities for visual communication projects, curated workshops, field visits, and structured interventions by media professionals.
